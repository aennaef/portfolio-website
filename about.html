About

My story

I’m a user experience designer and researcher, creating effective, data-driven solutions.
Using the right set of UX tools, I help drive smart business decisions by aligning goals and defining problems
before delivering effective, value-driven design solutions that make the digital experience more delightful for all users.

In the past, I earned a master’s in sociology focusing on research methods. I also worked in international education administration, advising students from all over the world about studying in Germany. In the civil service, I experienced how badly designed websites and applications hindered not only students' success but also my team's efficiency. I frequently found myself employing practical and creative methods that saved time, energy, and resources. My interest in sociology as well as in foreign affairs was motivated by a desire to understand and connect with individuals from different backgrounds. Through user-centered design, I am able to do just that.

Outside of work, you can find me caring for my +100 houseplants, sewing my own clothes, or buying more books than I have time to read.>
